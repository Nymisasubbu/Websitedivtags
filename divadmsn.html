Admissions

Eligibility
Minimum 60% in Intermediate or equivalent
Valid score in GAT / JEE / EAPCET (depending on the course)
Documents Required
1. 10th & 12th Certificates
2. Transfer Certificate
3. Passport-size Photos
4. Valid ID Proof
